```bash
cd /Users/utsavlamsal/Documents/MYBILLBOOK
```

## 📦 Add Your First Product (1 minute)

1. **Click "Inventory"** in the left sidebar
2. **Click "+ Add Product"** button
3. **Fill this form:**
   ```
   Product Name:      iPhone 15 Pro
   SKU Code:          IPHONE-15
   Category:          Electronics ✓
   Unit:              PCS ✓
   Sale Price:        180000
   Purchase Price:    155000
   Current Stock:     15
   Min Stock:         5
   Tax Rate:          5
   ```
4. **Click "Save Product"** 
5. **Success!** Add more products by repeating steps 2-4

---

## 👥 Add Your First Customer (1 minute)

1. **Click "Customers"** in the left sidebar
2. **Click "+ Add Customer"** button
3. **Fill this form:**
   ```
   Name:              Rajesh Kumar
   Email:             rajesh@gmail.com
   Phone:             9841234567
   Type:              Customer ✓
   GST (optional):    Leave blank
   Address:           Kathmandu
   ```
4. **Click "Save Customer"**
5. **Add more customers** by repeating

---

## 📄 Create Your First Invoice (2 minutes)

1. **Click "Billing"** in the left sidebar
2. **Click "+ Create Invoice"** button
3. **Select Customer:**
   - Click dropdown → Select from your customers
4. **Add Product:**
   - Click "Add Item"
   - Select product from dropdown
   - Enter quantity (e.g., 2)
   - Tax auto-calculates
5. **Set Payment Status:**
   - Mark as "Paid" or "Unpaid" or "Partial"
6. **Click "Save Invoice"**

---

## 🔧 Helpful Tips

### Keyboard Shortcuts
- **Tab** → Jump to next field
- **Enter** → Submit form
- **Escape** → Cancel

### Common Issues

**No products showing in invoice?**
→ Go Inventory, add at least 1 product

**Can't save invoice?**
→ Select a customer first

**App runs slow?**
→ Hard refresh: `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows)

**Lost data?**
→ Check your email for backup confirmation
